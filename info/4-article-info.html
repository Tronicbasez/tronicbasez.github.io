How to lose 20 kg

in a year

Fabionel Miguel 12/08/2017

social media part

You probably want to lose weight, but the question is how bad you want it! Before anything else, you gotta know that you might lose weight fast, but you also might get everything back, if stop having a healthy lifestyle.

Back in the days, I used to be the fat kid with neither self-esteem nor self-discipline. Due to a life-threatening situation, I had to change my outlook on life. I signed for judo class, which eventually, I ended up liking except the Mondays due to the crazy cardio routine.

I rebuilt my self-esteem, I stopped seeing food as my only source of pleasure because becoming good in my judo class was really rewarding. After few months, I could see the results in the mirror. If you want to lose weight and gain a lot of self-esteem and discipline, judo class are a good way to go. It's better than bodybuilding, judo athletes are bigger and stronger, just look at them. However, you have to reduce your food intake.

What happened when I stopped judo

Now that I'm not doing judo anymore, when I try to lose weight, I go through a fasting period until I feel really hungry, I mean really hungry which is different than feeling like eating. It usually takes one day to one and half day. After fasting I just eat oatmeal in the morning with tea and banana, chicken breast with salad in the afternoon when it's not chicken breast, it's fish with egg and salad, and soup in the evening. I keep like that for weeks until the adjusting phase, which is changing the diet according to the progress. In the beginning of the diet my workout consists of 35% cardio 40% bodylifting working out (the old school one), and 25% weight lifting, then I also go through the adjustment phase, when I lost a great amount of weight, I had to reduce cardio and body lifting and increase weights lifting.

The secret here is consistency, you gotta make it a routine. Everyday you gotta keep pushing yourself. Every morning you wake up you will be in a better position than the last morning.
